Best Restaurants in Saipan

Everyone, and I mean Everyone loves food. Food is not only what we need it's also culture and a good dining experience is great. In this article, I would like to give you the best restaurant you can find on the island of Saipan.

My 3 favorite Restaurants are:

  1. Surf Club
  2. J's Restaurant and Bar
  3. Cha Cafe

1. Surf's Club

Surf's Club has an amazing oceanview, food, and staff. you can get your traditional island mixed with American and Asian style food with an awesome ocean view at this Restaurant.This restaurant has a stand out items like fried lumpia, lemongrass chicken, loco moco, crab pasta, and tuna poke.


2. J's Restaurant

J's Restaurant and Bar is a really good place to eat if you are from The Philippines. This restaurant/bar has a great selection of Filipino Food like crispy pat and bulalo.


3. Cha Cafe

Cha Cafe is Saipan's Starbucks with its roasted coffee and freshly baked pastries. They have treats like cinnamon walnut twists, cinnamon rolls, blueberry tarts, garlic chicken sandwiches, and breakfast sandwiches. This place is a good spot to do relax because its so cozy and you can probably do school work at the same time.

Important Things to know
  • These restaurants are on google maps, which you can use as a way to get to one or all of these restaurants.
  • They all have social media, mainly Facebook or Instagram.
  • There are other restaurants but these I highly recommend.
